## Description (max 4000 characters)

CladLogic Released is a release-tracking platform built for facade fabrication teams. From CAD handoff to shop cut to field install, every release is tracked in one place — no spreadsheets, no chasing updates over email.

BUILT FOR YOUR WHOLE CREW
Every role in your operation has a purpose-built view. Project Managers set up projects and manage documents. CAD creates and manages releases. Shop tracks cuts, crates, and panel progress. Drivers and field crews see exactly what's shipping and where it stands.

RELEASE-FIRST TRACKING
Releases drive everything in CladLogic. Each release moves through a defined lifecycle — from CAD to shop to shipped — with clear status at every step. Nothing falls through the cracks.

SHIPMENT MILESTONES
Track crates from the shop floor to the job site. Shipment logistics have their own dedicated lane, completely separate from release status, so your drivers and field supervisors always know what's loaded and what's on the way.

DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT
Upload shop drawings, color approvals, QC photos, and more — all tied directly to the release they belong to. Every document is versioned and accessible to the right people at the right time.

ROLE-BASED ACCESS
Ten roles. Each one sees and does exactly what they should. Admins manage tenants and users. GMs control project lifecycle. CAD owns releases. Shop owns progress. Field owns install reports.

REAL-TIME STATUS
No more wondering where a release stands. Every team member sees current status the moment something changes.

CladLogic Released is a business application for commercial facade fabrication operations. An active company account is required.
